hritik vijay manjrekar

A youth in Indian politics paving way for himself with the help of his leadership skills and binding people with love care and respect. He has pursued Bachelor’s of commerce (2021) degree and desires to be a lawyer. He grafted his vision at the very beginning of his journey which started as a UNIT PRESIDENT, YUVA SENA COLLEGE KAKSHA, under the guidance of Shri. Varun Sardesai and at the same time was leading the YUVASENA SHAKHA - 67 as their PRESIDENT under the leadership of Shri. Aditya Uddhavji Thackeray (Cabinet Minister of Tourism and Environment, Maharashtra). His vision is to enhance the well-being of the society and to make sure that he inspires the youth to give back to the society that we are living in. He also runs an NGO, in the name of “All Heart Riders Group”, with a motive of giving a helping hand to the needy and underprivileged. He believes that, “Helping one person might not change the whole world but it could definitely change the world for that one person.”
